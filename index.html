Skills & Tools

User Empathy

Conducting user interviews to understand the needs of users guides my design process. Users can provide new wants and needs that might otherwise go unnoticed by designs.

Evaluative Research

Usability testing and heuristic analysis provide insight to the usability of an existing website or app during a redesign. Pairing a high-level review of the website from a designers perspective with annotations provided by users creates a well-rounded foundation of iterations to build upon.

Prototyping

Design iterations occur between user testing, developing from a low-fidelity design, to a mid-fidelity, and finally a high-fidelity prototype. Testing between iterations allows for additional user and stakeholder feedback and guidance on usability.
